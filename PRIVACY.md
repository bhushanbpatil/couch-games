# Privacy Policy — Couch Games

**Last updated:** July 5, 2026

Couch Games ("the app") is a local party-game app. We designed it so gameplay stays on your devices.

## Summary

- We do **not** collect, store, or sell personal data.
- We do **not** use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
- We do **not** require an account.

## Data the app uses locally

When you play, the app may store on your device only what is needed for that session, such as:

- Player names you type for a game
- In-game state (scores, roles, rounds)

This information stays on your phone and is cleared when you leave a game or close the app. We do not receive it on any server.

## Connect Play (nearby multiplayer)

If you use **Connect Play**, the app uses Apple's local networking features (Multipeer Connectivity) so phones near each other can join the same room. That communication is **device-to-device** on your local network or Bluetooth. It is encrypted by the system and is **not** sent to us or to any company server we operate.

iOS may ask permission to find devices on the local network. You can decline; Connect Play will not work without it. Pass & Play games work without local network access.

## Internet access

The app does not need the internet for gameplay. Links in the About screen (privacy policy, support) open only if you tap them.

## Children

The app is intended for general audiences playing together in person. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone.

## Changes

We may update this policy if the app changes. The latest version will be posted at this URL.
